Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what personal data HKOTC.io collects, how we use it, and your rights. We are committed to protecting your information in accordance with Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) and applicable international standards.

1. Who We Are

HKOTC.io ("we", "us", "our") is a cryptocurrency OTC (over-the-counter) exchange platform incorporated and operating in Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, People's Republic of China.

We act as the Data Controller for personal information collected through our website, mobile app, and services. Our registered office is located in Hong Kong.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@hkotc.io

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, nationality, government-issued ID (HKID, passport)
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, messaging handles (WhatsApp, Telegram)
  • Account data: Username, password (hashed), account preferences
  • Financial data: Bank account details, transaction history, trade records
  • KYC documents: ID photo, selfie, proof of address (required by AML regulations)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, referral source
  • Transaction metadata: Trade timestamps, wallet addresses, trade amounts

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Identity verification partners (KYC/AML providers)
  • Blockchain analytics platforms (for compliance screening)
  • Publicly available sources and sanctions lists

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Account management: Creating and maintaining your account, authentication and security
  • Trade execution: Processing your buy/sell orders, settlement, and receipts
  • KYC/AML compliance: Verifying your identity, screening against sanctions lists, meeting regulatory obligations
  • Customer support: Responding to your enquiries via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email
  • Communications: Sending rate alerts, trade confirmations, and service updates
  • Security: Detecting fraud, unauthorized access, and suspicious activity
  • Legal obligations: Complying with HKMA, FATF, and other applicable regulations
  • Service improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our platform

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to execute trades and provide services
  • Legal obligation: KYC/AML requirements under Hong Kong law
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention, security monitoring, service improvements
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional features (you may withdraw consent at any time)

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • KYC/identity verification providers
  • Banking and payment partners for HKD settlement
  • Cloud infrastructure and hosting providers
  • Customer support tooling

5.2 Legal & Regulatory

  • Hong Kong Police Force or regulatory authorities when lawfully required
  • HKMA or Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU) for AML/CFT reporting obligations
  • Courts or legal proceedings

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

All third parties are contractually bound to protect your data and may only use it for specified purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your account and provide services
  • Comply with AML/KYC record-keeping requirements (minimum 7 years under Hong Kong law)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • Strict access controls — staff access data on a need-to-know basis
  • 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

While we take every precaution, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and applicable authorities within the timeframes required by law.

8. Your Rights

Under Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data (PCPD)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hkotc.io or via WhatsApp/Telegram. We will respond within 40 days as required by the PDPO.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to operate our service (authentication, security). We use analytics cookies to understand usage patterns and improve our platform.

You can control cookies via your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to use our trading services.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell behavioral data to advertisers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notification at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Continued use of our services after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
